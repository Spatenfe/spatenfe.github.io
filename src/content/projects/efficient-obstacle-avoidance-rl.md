Bachelor's thesis at the Technical University of Munich on multi-agent reinforcement learning for efficient obstacle avoidance of vehicles, completed with a grade of 1.0. This work laid the foundation for the later publication at IEEE IV 2025.

## Highlights

- Developed a multi-agent reinforcement learning approach for vehicle obstacle avoidance.
- Graded 1.0.
- Formed the basis for the follow-up research published at IEEE IV 2025 (see "Decentralized RL for Multi-Agent Navigation").
